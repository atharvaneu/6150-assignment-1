    • About Us

    TYPEMASTER Keyboards started as a brand whose niche market was to cater to not only the gaming community but also the working professional. We specialize in mechanical keyboards and always suggest those over the old-school membrane based keyboards because of the type of experience they provide! Our goal is to keep the price reasonable but at the same time provide the best durability and quality a user can ask for - we are mainly able to do this because of our business partners Cherry MX and Gateron (psst. they provide us with the mechanical switches to build your keyboards)
